Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about planning your journey with us.

How does Skupheon build my itinerary?
expand_more
Skupheon uses your destination, travel dates, budget, and interests to generate a practical day-wise plan with places to visit, pacing recommendations, and activity flow tailored to your trip style.
Can I edit and save my generated trip plan?
expand_more
Yes. After generating a plan, you can save it to your account, revisit it from your trips section, and adjust details like dates, preferences, and destination choices as your plan evolves.
How do notifications and reminders work?
expand_more
Notification settings let you turn alerts on or off and choose types such as trip alerts, AI suggestions, system updates, and seasonal recommendations. You can manage everything from your settings page.
Does RoutheonSkups provide weather and seasonal insights?
expand_more
Yes. The platform includes destination weather support and season-aware suggestions so you can make better decisions about what to do, when to travel, and how to structure each day comfortably.
Is my trip data private in RoutheonSkups?
expand_more
Your saved trips, profile preferences, and itinerary data are account-based and intended only for your planning experience. We use this information to improve personalization inside your own dashboard.
