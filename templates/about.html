What is RoutheonSkups?

Your Smart Travel Companion

RoutheonSkups is an innovative web-based travel planning application designed specifically for the Indian market. Using advanced AI technology, we help travelers discover destinations, create personalized itineraries, and plan memorable trips.

Our platform uses Groq AI API to provide intelligent responses and recommendations, making travel planning effortless and personalized.

Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or a month-long adventure, RoutheonSkups helps you create the perfect travel plan tailored to your budget, time, and interests.
